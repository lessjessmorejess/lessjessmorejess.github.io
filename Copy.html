Jessie Leslie
My art focuses on the striking similarities between nature and technology. There are parallels between natural selection and the way we create artificial intelligence, the veins of a city and roots of a tree, computer viruses and garden weeds. These two things are constantly put at odds against one another, yet they are closer to a Yin and Yang.
I find that these kinds of comparisons can be found anywhere if you boil out the eccentricities. These core relationships between “radically different” things are worth gushing over. I also find it interesting how, when isolated together, these relationships spark meaning or story in my viewers and myself. This is also evident in my mixing of mediums through collaging water color and acrylic paints, inking, and stippling together. Their contrast cover the weaknesses and limitations of each other, but also still all run on the same artistic philosophies.
